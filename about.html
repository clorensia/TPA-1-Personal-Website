About Candra Lorensia Ardiyanti

I love building with data and crafting the technologngs insights to life. I am currently sharpening my skills on both ends of the stack, pursuing my degree in Data Science while immersing myself in a rigorous Full Stack Developmer bootcamp by Perempuan Inovasi.

My goal is to become a versatile builder who can own a project from a data hypothesis all the way to a deployed product.

I'm currently seeking opportunities where I can apply this unique blend of analytical rigor and full-stack craftsmanship to create impactful, data-driven solutions.
